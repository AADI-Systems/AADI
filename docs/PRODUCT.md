# AADI Product

Version: 1.0.1  
Date: July 2026  

> AI should prepare decisions, not replace human decisions.

---

# 1. Product Overview

AADI (Autonomous AI Decision Intelligence) is a Decision Intelligence Platform that helps people prepare better decisions.

AADI transforms fragmented information into clear, explainable, prioritized, and actionable Decision Briefs.

AADI does not make decisions for people.

AADI prepares people to make better decisions.

---

# 2. Product Vision

Modern professionals are surrounded by increasing amounts of information.

The challenge is no longer only finding information.

The real challenge is:

- Understanding what matters.
- Identifying important signals.
- Recognizing risks.
- Evaluating possible options.
- Preparing before decisions become urgent.

AADI aims to become a Decision Preparation Layer between information and human judgment.


Information

↓

AADI Decision Preparation Layer

↓

Human Decision


---

# 3. Problem Definition

People often make poor decisions not because they lack information.

They fail because:

- Information is fragmented.
- Important signals are hidden.
- Risks are discovered too late.
- Cognitive load reduces decision quality.

The problem is not simply information overload.

The deeper problem is:

> Cognitive overload.

AADI reduces cognitive load by helping people understand:

- What matters most.
- Why it matters.
- What risks exist.
- What options should be considered.
- What should be prepared next.

# 8. Decision Brief

The core output of AADI is the Decision Brief.

A Decision Brief is not:

- A summary.
- A chatbot answer.
- A final recommendation.

A Decision Brief is:

> A structured preparation document that helps humans understand situations and prepare better decisions.

A Decision Brief contains:

- Situation Assessment
- Priority
- Evidence
- Reasoning
- Risk Assessment
- Options
- Recommended Preparation
- Missing Information
- Confidence
- References
