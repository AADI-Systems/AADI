# AADI Roadmap

Version: 1.0  
Date: July 2026  

> Building a Decision Intelligence Platform step by step.

---

# Overview

AADI development follows one principle:

> Build the right foundation before adding more capabilities.

The roadmap focuses on three evolution stages:


Decision Intelligence

↓

Knowledge & Collaboration

↓

Enterprise Decision Intelligence


---

# Phase 1 — Decision Intelligence Core

## Goal

Establish the foundation of AADI:

> Helping individuals prepare better decisions.

---

## Core Capabilities

### Decision Brief

Create structured Decision Briefs that transform fragmented information into decision preparation.

Includes:

- Situation Assessment
- Evidence
- Reasoning
- Risk Assessment
- Options
- Missing Information
- Confidence

---

### Reasoning

Develop reasoning capabilities that help users:

- Understand situations.
- Identify important factors.
- Explore relationships.
- Evaluate uncertainty.

---

### Decision Preparation Workflow

Establish the core workflow:


Information

↓

Understanding

↓

Reasoning

↓

Risk Assessment

↓

Priority

↓

Decision Brief

↓

Human Decision


---

## Success Criteria

Users can:

- Understand complex information faster.
- Identify important issues.
- Prepare before making decisions.

---

# Phase 2 — Knowledge & Memory

## Goal

Transform AADI from a decision preparation tool into a continuous intelligence system.

---

## Core Capabilities

### Knowledge

Build structured knowledge capabilities.

Enable AADI to understand:

- Historical information.
- Domain context.
- Previous decisions.

---

### Memory

Create decision memory.

Allow AADI to remember:

- Previous analysis.
- Past situations.
- Decision patterns.

---

### Multiple Reasoning Capabilities

Expand reasoning through specialized capabilities.

Examples:

- Analytical reasoning.
- Risk reasoning.
- Opportunity analysis.
- Scenario evaluation.

---

## Success Criteria

AADI can provide:

- Context-aware preparation.
- More consistent analysis.
- Better understanding over time.

---

# Phase 3 — Enterprise Decision Intelligence

## Goal

Bring Decision Intelligence into organizations.

---

## Core Capabilities

### Workspace

Enable teams to:

- Share Decision Briefs.
- Collaborate on analysis.
- Maintain organizational knowledge.

---

### Integrations

Connect AADI with:

- Enterprise data sources.
- Business systems.
- Existing workflows.

---

### Enterprise Decision Intelligence

Support organizations in:

- Improving decision preparation.
- Reducing information gaps.
- Preserving organizational knowledge.

---

## Success Criteria

Organizations can:

- Prepare decisions collaboratively.
- Reduce cognitive load at scale.
- Improve decision quality.

---

# Long-Term Vision

AADI evolves from:


Individual Decision Preparation

↓

Team Decision Intelligence

↓

Enterprise Decision Intelligence


The goal is not replacing human decisions.

The goal is:

> Helping humans make better decisions through better preparation.

---

# Development Principle

Every roadmap item must answer:

> Does this help people prepare a better decision?

If Yes:

Build it.

If No:

Do not build it.
