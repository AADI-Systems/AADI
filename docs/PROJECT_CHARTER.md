# AADI Project Charter

Version: 1.0  
Date: July 2026  

> AI should prepare decisions, not replace human decisions.

---

# 1. Purpose

AADI (Autonomous AI Decision Intelligence) exists to help people prepare better decisions.

Modern professionals are surrounded by increasing amounts of information, but the fundamental challenge is not lack of information.

The real limitation is human cognitive capacity.

People have limited:

- Attention
- Time
- Ability to process complexity
- Ability to identify what matters most

AADI exists to reduce cognitive load by transforming fragmented information into structured, explainable, prioritized, and actionable Decision Briefs.

AADI does not make decisions for humans.

AADI prepares humans to make better decisions.

---

# 4. Problem Definition

The problem AADI solves is not:

> Information overload.

The deeper problem is:

> Cognitive overload.

People often fail not because they cannot find information.

They fail because:

- They cannot identify what matters most.
- They cannot evaluate competing factors.
- They cannot recognize risks early.
- They cannot prepare before decisions become urgent.

AADI focuses on improving decision preparation.

# 6. Decision Brief Standard

Decision Brief is the core product output of AADI.

A Decision Brief should contain:

- Situation Assessment
- Priority
- Evidence
- Reasoning
- Risk Assessment
- Options
- Recommended Preparation
- Missing Information
- Confidence
- References

---

A Decision Brief does not answer:

> What decision should you make?

It answers:

> What should you understand and prepare before making this decision?

# 18. Definition of Success

- People use AADI before important decisions.
- Decision Briefs improve decision preparation.
- Users understand situations faster.
- Cognitive load is reduced.
- Reasoning becomes clearer.
- Humans make better-informed decisions.

Success is not measured by:

- Number of AI features
- Number of agents
- Model complexity

Success is measured by:

> The quality of human decisions improved by better preparation.
